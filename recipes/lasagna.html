Lasagna


Best Lasagna Ever!

This is simply the best lasagna recipe, handed down from my mother to you good people! Use the best ingredients that you can find and you will not be disappointed.


Ingredients

  • dried lasagna sheets
  • pasta sauce made with italian sausage
  • ricotta cheese
  • mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta sheets
  2. Layer pasta sheets, pasta sauce, and cheese
  3. Cook in the oven at 350 until browned and bubbly
  4. Enjoy!
