Fried Chicken


Best Fried Chicken Ever!

This is simply the best fried chicken recipe, handed down from my mother to you good people! Use the best ingredients that you can find and you will not be disappointed.


Ingredients

  • 2-3lb chicken cut up for frying
  • Buttermilk
  • Seasoned flour
  • Vegetable shortening

Steps

  1. Marinade chicken in buttermilk
  2. Coat in seasoned flour
  3. Fry in vegetable shortening at 350 until golden brown and delicious
  4. Enjoy!
